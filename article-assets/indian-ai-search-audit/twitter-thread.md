## Tweet 4 (FINDING 2)

```
The bigger structural finding: there's a 40-percentage-point gap between branded and unbranded queries.

Branded comparison queries ("X vs Y") surface 73% of the time.

Unbranded category queries ("best X for Y") surface only 33%.

The actual AI-search battle is unbranded.
```

---

## Tweet 5 (FINDING 3 — Snitch case)

```
AIO and ChatGPT are not the same product.

Snitch surfaces in 10% of AIO queries but 90% of ChatGPT queries — an 80-percentage-point gap on the same brand.

Pristyn Care is the inverse: 80% AIO, 30% ChatGPT.

Per-platform optimization is real and necessary.
```

## Tweet 6 (FINDING 4 — newer-funded match established)
