## Posting cadence + best practice

- **Best time to post**: Mon-Wed 8-11 AM ET (highest LinkedIn engagement for B2B). For Sat ship: post Sat morning IST (Fri evening ET) so it hits the US Monday morning algorithm.
- **First 60 minutes are critical**: reply to every comment in the first hour, ask 3-5 people in your network to engage immediately (DM 5 minutes before post: "posting in 5, would love your perspective if you have a sec")
- **DON'T use linkedin native scheduler for carousels** if there's a known engagement bug — post manually if needed
- **Pin the carousel** to your profile for 30 days after posting

## Production options

- **Canva** — quickest, free, 1080×1350 carousel template exists
- **Figma** — best control, brand consistency, 1-2 hours to design 10 slides
- **Buffer/Hootsuite carousel creators** — middle option
- **Hand to Opus** — if Opus PDF pipeline includes carousel export, same data feeds both

Recommendation: Opus produces both the PDF and the carousel from the same brand
template; carousel JPEGs export at 1080×1350 from the same source.
