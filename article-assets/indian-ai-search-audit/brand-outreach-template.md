## Personalization tips before sending

- **Look up each marketing lead on LinkedIn** — first name in the salutation
- **Note any recent move/role change** — adjust opening ("congrats on the new role")
- **If you have a direct intro through someone** — name them in line 1
- **For brands with low surface rates** — frame as opportunity, not failure
- **For brands with high surface rates** — frame as benchmark, not flattery

## Sending cadence

- **Wed-Thu (3 days before publish)**: send to top 8 brands you most want share-back from
- **Fri (1 day before publish)**: send to remaining 17
- Don't send all 25 in one day — Gmail may flag bulk-similar content as spam
